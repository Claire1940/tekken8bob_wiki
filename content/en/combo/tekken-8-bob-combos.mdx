In Tekken 8, Bob returns with a heroic yet humorous narrative as the **Onigiri Savior**. Following a lengthy hospitalization resulting from his confrontation with Bryan Fury, Bob rejected the bland recovery meals provided to him and instead developed his own high-calorie hamburger recipe. This culinary venture blossomed into a highly successful fast-food chain, which he later expanded by launching Bob's Onigiri.

When G Corp launched its invasion of New York under Kazuya Mishima, Bob utilized his extensive food distribution network to provide free hamburgers and onigiri to citizens in need. His heroic relief efforts earned him his community nickname. Recognizing his dedication to justice, the Manhattan District Attorney's Office appointed him as an emergency investigator, tasking him with bringing Kazuya to justice.
