<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System and Dash Conversions">

The Heat system in Tekken 8 transforms Bob into a relentless juggernaut. His Heat Engagers, such as the new option from Spinner Ball, allow him to close the distance instantly with significant frame advantage. Mastering the **tekken 8 bob combo** in Heat requires a deep understanding of the just-frame Heat Smash.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat via a **Heat Burst** for a quick tactical reset or land a **Heat Engager** to gain an immediate offensive rush. Spinner Ball now includes a dedicated Heat Engager for Season 3.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Heat Dash Conversion">
    After a successful Heat Engager, trigger a **Heat Dash**. This consumes your Heat gauge but allows you to continue a combo that would otherwise end, or maintain pressure on a blocking opponent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Just-Frame Heat Smash">
    Execute Bob's Heat Smash with precise timing. A successful **just-frame input** increases the total damage and triggers a unique cinematic animation, making it his most powerful "cash-out" tool.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Season 3 Adjustments">
    Be aware that Season 3 has removed the wall splat behavior from Heat Smashes for most characters. Focus on using Heat Smash for pure damage or to erase the opponent's recoverable health.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Heat Strategy">
During Heat, Bob gains additional follow-up attacks that help mitigate his harder punishment execution. Use this window to play more aggressively against characters with superior defensive frames.
</Callout>
