<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System and Just-Frame Mechanics">

The Heat system is where Bob truly shines in Season 3. His Heat plan focuses on grounded combat and relentless stance-based pressure. The most discussed feature of his Heat mode is the **Just-Frame Heat Smash**. Unlike standard Heat Smashes that only require a button press, Bob's version rewards precise timing.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Heat via Engager">
    Use **Spinner Ball** or a standard Heat Engager to enter the Heat state with a forward dash. This puts you in an immediate pressure situation with a frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Heat Dash">
    Spend your Heat gauge to trigger a **Heat Dash** after a landed attack. This extends your combo or allows you to stay in the opponent's face if the move was blocked.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Just-Frame Smash">
    When the opponent is vulnerable, trigger the Heat Smash. Tap the final input with precise timing to trigger the alternate animation and deal **20% more damage**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Heat Tool | Effect | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Heat Burst** | Standard Activation | Use as a safe way to reset neutral. |
| **Heat Dash** | Combo Extender | Crucial for reaching the wall from midscreen. |
| **Heat Smash** | High Damage Finisher | Best used when the opponent is at 30% health. |
| **Recoverable Gauge** | Health Sustain | Bob gains health faster during Heat pressure. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Tip">
