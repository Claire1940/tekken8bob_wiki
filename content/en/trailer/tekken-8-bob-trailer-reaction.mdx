<Section icon={Target} title="Heat System Guide and Combo Routes">

The Heat system is the heart of Tekken 8, and Bob utilizes it to mitigate his traditional weakness: difficult punishment execution. By entering Heat, Bob gains access to tools that simplify his offense and increase his damage floor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat via **Heat Burst** for a safe mid attack or use a **Heat Engager** like the new Spinner Ball follow-up to rush down the opponent with a massive frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grounded Pressure">
    While in Heat, Bob's grounded attacks gain chip damage and improved recovery. Focus on United Step mix-ups to force the opponent into making a mistake.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Just-Frame Smash">
    When the Heat gauge is low, execute the **Heat Smash**. Aim for the just-frame timing (precise button press) to trigger the alternate animation and maximum damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Heat Dash Conversion">
    Use **Heat Dash** after a blocked Heat Engager to stay in the opponent's face, or use it after a hit to convert into a full aerial combo.
  </Step>
</Steps>
