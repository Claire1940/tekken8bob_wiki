### The Spinner Ball System
Bob's **Spinner Ball** has received the most significant overhaul in **Tekken 8**. It can now be used as an aerial combo extender and features a dedicated Heat Engager option.

- **Spinner Ball into Heat**: Activating Heat through this stance allows Bob to stay "sticky" on the opponent, forcing constant guesses between high-damage mids and fast lows.
- **Aerial Utility**: Unlike previous entries, Bob can now transition into Spinner Ball mid-air to increase wall-carry distance or adjust his landing recovery.

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Tactic">
