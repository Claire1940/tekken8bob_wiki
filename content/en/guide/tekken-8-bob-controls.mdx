<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tekken 8 Bob Heat System and Just-Frame Timing">

The Heat System in Tekken 8 significantly buffs Bob's grounded combat capabilities. When Bob enters Heat, he gains access to enhanced versions of his Freestyle Karate moves and a unique finisher that rewards player precision.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat via **Heat Burst** for a quick smash or a **Heat Engager** (like his new Spinner Ball option) to rush forward with a massive frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maintaining Pressure">
    During Heat, Bob's moves generate more chip damage. Use his United Step follow-ups to force the opponent to block, slowly whittling down their health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Heat Dash Conversion">
    Use a **Heat Dash** after a landed Heat Engager to extend your combo. In Season 3, Heat Dash scaling has been adjusted, so focus on stable carry rather than maximum raw damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Heat Smash Finisher">
    Execute Bob's Heat Smash to cash out your remaining gauge. This move consumes the entire bar but delivers devastating damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Bob's **Heat Smash** is unique because it features a **just-frame input**. If you time the final button press perfectly, the animation changes to a more cinematic version, and the damage output is significantly boosted. This makes Bob one of the few characters where "lab time" directly translates to increased damage potential.

| Tool | Type | Effect in Heat |

| **Heat Smash** | Finisher | High damage; Just-frame increases power. |
| **Giga Jacker** | Power Crush | Faster startup and better recovery in Heat. |
