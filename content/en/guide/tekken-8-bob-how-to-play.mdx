The Heat System in Tekken 8 significantly buffs Bob’s grounded combat. In Season 3, system changes have adjusted how Heat Smash interacts with walls, making Bob's open-stage pressure even more vital. His Heat Engagers are primarily tied to his stances, encouraging a flow-state playstyle.

  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat via **Heat Burst** for a safe activation or use **Heat Engagers** like the new Spinner Ball transition to gain an immediate frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stance Buffs">
    While in Heat, Bob gains additional follow-up attacks from **Spinner Ball**. This stance now includes a built-in Heat Engager that forces the opponent into a defensive guess.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Heat Dash Conversions">
    Use **Heat Dash** after a blocked or hit Heat Engager to close the gap. In Season 3, this is your primary tool for extending pressure without committing to a full Smash.

  <Step num="4" title="The Just-Frame Smash">

  </Step>
</Steps>

Fighting against Bob requires discipline. His large frame is a double-edged sword; while he has great reach, he can be vulnerable to precise lateral movement. Most of his high-pressure stance entries can be interrupted if you recognize the startup animations early.

| Opponent Type | Bob's Advantage | Strategy to Counter Bob | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rushdown** | Giga Jacker interrupts | Sidestep right to avoid United Step | Medium |

| **Grapplers** | Agile movement escapes | Break his generic throws; watch for reversals | Low |
| **All-Rounders** | Superior Heat tools | Punish his whiffed Spinner Ball attempts | Medium |
