<Section icon={Target} title="Tekken 8 Bob Beginner Guide">

Starting with **Tekken 8 Bob** requires a shift in mindset. While his physical frame suggests a slow, "big-body" character, he plays more like an agile all-rounder. Beginners should focus on his grounded pressure and universal Tekken 8 systems before attempting his more difficult just-frame execution.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Embrace the Speed and Weight Identity">
    Do not play Bob like a traditional heavy character. Use his agile movement to stay in the close-to-mid range. His Freestyle Karate is most effective when you are close enough to force the opponent to guess between his fast mids and crushing lows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Heat System Basics">
    Bob relies heavily on Heat to cover his execution-heavy punishment game. Use Heat Burst to reset the neutral or Heat Engagers like the new Spinner Ball follow-up to rush down opponents with a significant frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Special Style for Learning">
    If you are new to the franchise, toggle Special Style to see how Bob's core moves flow together. This helps you understand the timing of his aerial carry and the reach of his Power Crushes without memorizing complex inputs immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Practice the Just-Frame Heat Smash">
    Bob's Heat Smash is unique because it rewards precise timing. Spend time in practice mode hitting the just-frame input. Successful timing increases the damage and triggers a specialized animation that can close out rounds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
