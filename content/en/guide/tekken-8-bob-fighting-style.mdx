A fundamental pillar of the **tekken 8 bob fighting style** is his reliance on two versatile stances. These stances allow Bob to transition from a neutral state into high-pressure mix-ups seamlessly. In the 2026 Season 3 update, these stances have been granted new properties that interact with the recoverable health system.

**United Step** remains Bob’s primary pressure tool. It allows him to close the distance while maintaining access to signature moves like **Langue Washer** and **Cutting Coppa**. **Spinner Ball**, on the other hand, has seen significant buffs in *Tekken 8*. It can now be used as an aerial combo extension, making Bob's wall carry more formidable than ever. More importantly, Spinner Ball is now a gateway to his Heat system.
