<Section icon={Swords} title="Heat System and Rage Strategy">

The Heat system in Tekken 8 is where Bob truly shines. His Heat tools are designed to cover his primary weakness: difficult punishment execution. When Heat is active, Bob gains additional follow-up attacks that simplify his offense and increase his lethality in grounded combat.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat via **Heat Burst** for a safe activation or land a **Heat Engager** (like the new Spinner Ball follow-up) to rush in with a massive frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Applying Pressure">
    Use **Heat Dash** after a blocked Heat Engager to stay in the opponent's face. This forces a mix-up situation where Bob's mid/low game becomes oppressive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Just-Frame Smash">
    Bob's **Heat Smash** is his ultimate "cash-out" tool. Timing the input perfectly (just-frame) increases the damage and triggers a unique, high-impact animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rage Management">
    Once health drops below 25%, Rage activates. Balance the use of your **Rage Art** to erase the opponent's recoverable health, but be wary as Bob's Rage Art is highly punishable if blocked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Season 3 System Changes">
Note that in Season 3, Heat Smash no longer causes a wall splat. Focus on using Heat Dash for combo extensions or using the just-frame Heat Smash for raw damage in the open field.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Essential Combos and Punishment">

Effective punishment is the hallmark of a great Bob player. While his execution can be demanding, his reward for a successful "launch punish" is among the highest in the game. Beginners should focus on stable, high-reliability routes before attempting advanced Spinner Ball aerial extensions.

| Situation | Recommended Move | Follow-up Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **10f Punish** | Jab Series | Basic chip damage and frame advantage |
| **12f Punish** | Mid-check | Keep the opponent grounded and reset neutral |
| **15f (Launch)** | Main Launcher | Transition into full aerial carry |
| **Power Crush** | **Giga Jacker** | Absorb high/mid attacks and knock down |

**Basic Combo Structure:**
1. **Starter**: Landing a launcher or a counter-hit.
2. **Carry**: Using agile pokes to move the opponent toward the wall.
3. **Stance Extension**: Incorporating **Spinner Ball** for extra hits in the air.
4. **Ender**: A heavy hit or a Heat Dash conversion for maximum damage.

<Callout type="info" title="Combo Tip">
Bob's Spinner Ball is now a core component of his aerial routing. Practice transitioning from a standard launch into a Spinner Ball Roulette Cutter to maximize your wall-carry distance.
</Callout>
