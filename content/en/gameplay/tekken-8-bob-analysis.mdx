### Key Move Analysis

Bob's moveset in Tekken 8 balances returning classics with brand-new tools designed for the "Aggressive" gameplay philosophy.

- **Giga Jacker**: A fast-starting Power Crush. Essential for breaking through opponent pressure when you are at a frame disadvantage.
- **Onigiri Bomber**: A new high-impact strike tied to his story. It serves as a reliable mid-range check with excellent pushback on block.
- **Langue Washer**: Returning from United Step, this low attack remains a staple for opening up defensive opponents.
- **Roulette Cutter**: Now part of the expanded Spinner Ball toolkit, providing a powerful ender for wall carry combos.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System Integration & Combo Routes">

The Heat system is where Bob truly shines. His ability to maintain pressure while in Heat makes him one of the most dangerous characters in Season 3.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat using **Heat Burst** for a safe activation or land a **Heat Engager** (like the new Spinner Ball transition) to rush forward with a massive frame advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grounded Pressure">
    While in Heat, Bob gains additional follow-up attacks. Use these to chip away at the opponent's recoverable health and force them to stop ducking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Heat Dash Conversion">
    Use **Heat Dash** after a blocked Heat Engager to stay in the opponent's face, or use it after a hit to extend a combo that would otherwise drop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Just-Frame Heat Smash">

  </Step>
