<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System Mechanics and Just-Frame Rewards">

The Heat System in Tekken 8 drastically changes how Bob approaches a round. Unlike previous iterations where Bob relied on steady chipping, the Heat state allows him to force devastating mix-ups. One of the most unique features revealed in the **Tekken 8 Bob** first look is his **Just-Frame Heat Smash**. 

When Bob activates his Heat Smash, players have a small window to input a follow-up command. If timed correctly, the move deals significantly more damage and triggers a unique animation where Bob utilizes his weight for a crushing finisher.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Heat Activation">
    Enter Heat via **Heat Burst** for a neutral reset or a **Heat Engager** (like his new Spinner Ball transition) to gain a massive frame advantage on hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Pressure Maintenance">
    During Heat, Bob gains access to enhanced "chip damage" on his heavy Karate strikes. Use this to whittle down the opponent's recoverable health even if they are blocking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Just-Frame Cashout">
    Execute the **Heat Smash** when the opponent is near the end of their health bar. Focus on the visual flash to hit the just-frame input for maximum damage output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Season 3 System Changes">
Note that in Season 3, Heat Smashes no longer cause a wall splat. Focus on using your Heat Dash to extend combos toward the wall, rather than relying on the Smash for positioning.
</Callout>

</Section>
