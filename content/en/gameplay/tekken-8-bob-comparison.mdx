<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System Integration and Season 3 Dynamics">

The Heat System is the defining mechanic of Tekken 8, and Bob utilizes this system to its absolute limit. Entering Heat unlocks a variety of enhanced properties for his standard attacks, turning his already respectable grounded offense into an oppressive wall of block pressure. His Heat Engagers are strategically distributed across his most reliable neutral pokes, ensuring he can transition into his powered-up state naturally during combat.

In Season 3, system-wide adjustments have refined how Heat Dash and Heat Smash behave. Bob's Heat Smash is particularly unique due to its just-frame input requirement. Executing the Heat Smash with perfect timing results in a massive damage boost and triggers an alternate, highly cinematic animation that leaves the opponent structurally disadvantaged even if survived.

In the early Season 3 meta, Bob profiles as a formidable A-tier contender. His exceptional mobility, paired with his high-density grounded pressure, makes him incredibly difficult to contain.
