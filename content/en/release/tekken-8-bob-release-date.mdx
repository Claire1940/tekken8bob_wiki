<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Progression and Heat System Guide">

Starting out with Bob in Tekken 8 requires a solid grasp of his unique "Speed & Weight" design. Unlike traditional large-frame characters who rely solely on slow, high-damage single hits, Bob plays more like a lightweight rushdown character with heavy-body hitboxes. This makes him highly accessible for beginners, though mastering his optimal execution takes dedication.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Close-Range Dominance">
    Focus on grounded combat. Use fast mid-level jabs and quick low pokes to force your opponent to block, creating opportunities to transition into your stance pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Stance Flow">
    Practice shifting smoothly between United Step and Spinner Ball. Understanding when to transition from a low sweep into a Spinner Ball mix-up is the key to cracking tough defenses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate the Heat State">
    Trigger your Heat state early in the round using a Heat Burst or a landed Heat Engager. This unlocks Bob's advanced offensive tools and helps cover his harder defensive punishes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Practice Just-Frame Timing">
    Spend time in Training Mode practicing the exact button release window for Bob's Heat Smash. Perfecting this timing increases your damage output significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Recoverable Health">
    Keep the pressure on when your health is low. Bob's aggressive stance options are highly effective at chipping away at the opponent's guard and restoring your recoverable health.
  </Step>
</Steps>
