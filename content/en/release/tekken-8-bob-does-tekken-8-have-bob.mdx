<Section icon={Target} title="Heat System Integration & Season 3 Balance Rules">

The Heat system is the defining mechanic of Tekken 8, and Bob's implementation is designed to enhance his aggressive close-range game plan. Entering Heat grants Bob enhanced properties on his stance transitions, access to unique follow-up strikes, and a highly rewarding high-damage Heat Smash.

Under current Season 3 balance rules, system-wide changes have adjusted how Heat interactions function. Players must manage their Heat gauge carefully, as excessive wall-splat scenarios and certain Heat Dash advantages have been standardized to promote tactical, neutral-focused gameplay.
