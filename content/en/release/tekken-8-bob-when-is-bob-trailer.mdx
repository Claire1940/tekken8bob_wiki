<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System and Rage Integration">

Tekken 8 introduces the Heat System, and Bob benefits significantly from these mechanics. His Heat state enhances his grounded combat and provides a unique reward for players with high technical skill.

**Heat System Features:**
- **Heat Engagers**: Moves like the Spinner Ball follow-up activate Heat on hit, rushing Bob forward with a significant frame advantage.
- **Heat Dash**: After a Heat Engager, Bob can expend his gauge to continue a combo or force a guessing game on block.
- **Just-Frame Heat Smash**: Bob's Heat Smash is unique. If you time the final input perfectly, the damage increases, and he performs an alternate, more cinematic animation.

<Callout type="success" title="Season 3 System Changes">
Note that in Season 3, Heat Smash wall splat behavior has been adjusted. Bob players should focus on using Heat Dash for wall carry rather than relying solely on the Heat Smash for splats.
</Callout>
