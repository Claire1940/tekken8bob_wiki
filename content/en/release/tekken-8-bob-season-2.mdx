<Section icon={Swords} title="Move List, United Step, and Spinner Ball">

Bob's combat style is built entirely around Freestyle Karate, combining powerful heavy-frame attacks with physics-defying agility. His core strategy relies on close- to mid-range grounded pressure, forcing opponents to respect his fast mid attacks and tricky low options. 

Key returning stances such as **United Step** and **Spinner Ball** allow Bob to maintain constant offensive flow. United Step features signature attacks like Langue Washer and Cutting Coppa, which restrict the opponent's defensive movement and create high-damage counter-hit opportunities. Meanwhile, Spinner Ball has been expanded in Tekken 8 to include direct transitions from low attacks and new Heat Engager properties.

| Move Name | Move Type | Gameplay Function | Stance Transition |
| Giga Jacker | Power Crush | Fast-starting armor move to absorb attacks | None |
| Perfect Body | Attack Reversal | Defensive parry tool to reverse pressure | None |
| Langue Washer | United Step Attack | Signature pressure tool to restrict options | United Step |
| Cutting Coppa | United Step Attack | High-damage follow-up on counter-hit | United Step |
| Roulette Cutter | Spinner Ball Attack | Expanded aerial attack for combo extensions | Spinner Ball |
| Onigiri Bomber | New Attack | Lore-inspired forward strike with high impact | None |

<Callout type="info" title="Defensive Turn-Stealing">
With Giga Jacker acting as a fast Power Crush and Perfect Body serving as a new Attack Reversal, Bob has some of the strongest defensive response tools in the game. Use these options when opponents overextend their pressure.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Heat System Guide & Rage Art">

The Heat system in Tekken 8 significantly enhances Bob's grounded offensive capabilities. Activating Heat grants Bob access to additional follow-up attacks that help cover his harder-to-execute punishment game. His primary Heat Engager is tied directly to his Spinner Ball stance, allowing him to transition seamlessly from movement to full offensive advantage.

One of Bob's standout mechanics is his **just-frame Heat Smash**. Executing this move with precise timing increases its overall damage output and triggers an alternate, highly cinematic animation. Additionally, his Rage Art features a comical, dynamic presentation where he utilizes his "perfect body" to crush opponents.

<Section icon={Target} title="Combo Routes & Wall Carry Strategies">

Bob's combo routing in Tekken 8 is highly flexible, allowing players to convert simple ground pokes into massive wall-carry sequences. His primary combo extensions rely on transitioning into Spinner Ball mid-air, using moves like Roulette Cutter to keep the opponent suspended. 

Under Season 3 balance rules, Heat Smash wall splats have been adjusted across all characters. Therefore, Bob players must focus on using standard wall-carry tools to set up grounded pressure and United Step mix-ups rather than relying solely on Heat Smash cashouts.

| Combo Type | Key Launcher | Stance Extension | Primary Goal | Difficulty |
| Standard Launch | Ground Launcher | Spinner Ball Aerial | Consistent carry and damage | Easy |
| Aerial Extension | Air Launcher | Spinner Ball Roulette Cutter | High-damage wall-carry routing | Medium |
| Heat Conversion | Heat Engager | Heat Dash | Block pressure or combo extension | Medium |
| Low Transition | Low Attack | Spinner Ball | Open defense and continue pressure | Hard |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combo Stability Over Damage">
For beginners, prioritize stable aerial carry over complex stance transitions. Bob's basic Freestyle Karate strikes deal high base damage, making stable combos highly rewarding even without advanced Spinner Ball extensions.
</Callout>
